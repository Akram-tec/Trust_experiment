Welcome to the Trust and Investment Decision-Making Experiment.

In this study, you will take part in an interactive game that simulates economic exchanges between a company and a community. You will be paired with another participant and assigned a role at random: either as a representative of a company (Player A) or as a representative of a community (Player B).

Each of you will make decisions that impact not only your own outcomes but also those of your paired partner. These decisions will involve distributing a certain number of tokens, which hold real monetary value and will be converted into dollars at the experiment's conclusion. Your total earnings will consist of a participation fee, plus any additional money accumulated based on your decisions during the game. Higher the points earned higher the end payoff.

  1. The interaction will consist of several rounds, each requiring a decision on how to use your points.
  2. All decisions are anonymous, and the identities of participants will not be disclosed.
  3. It is important to consider both short-term gains and long-term strategy throughout the game.
  4. Roles will be assigned after the instructions.

There are no right or wrong answers, so we encourage you to act as you would naturally in each situation presented to you.
